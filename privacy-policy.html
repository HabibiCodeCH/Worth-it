Privacy Policy

Worth It? Chrome Extension — Last updated: June 2026

Summary

Worth It? does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What data is stored

The extension stores the following in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local):

  • Your hourly rate (derived from the salary you enter)
  • Your chosen currency
  • Whether the extension is enabled or paused

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server, and no one — including the developer — can access it.

Network activity

Worth It? makes zero network requests. It has no backend, no analytics, no telemetry, and no third-party services. The extension works entirely offline.

Permissions

The extension requests the storage permission solely to save your preferences locally. It does not request access to your browsing history, identity, or any other sensitive data.

Third parties

No data is shared with third parties. The extension does not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, or any external services.

Children

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. The extension version number in the Chrome Web Store will reflect any significant change.
